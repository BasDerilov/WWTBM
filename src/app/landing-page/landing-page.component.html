Welcome to "Who wants to be a millionaire"

In this game you will get the chance to show your knowledge in various trivia and get rich while doing so.

The rules are simple, you will get a question with four possible answers, choosing the correct answer will allow you to advance to the next level. The game is divided in 3 difficulty stages, at the beginning of each you you have the chance to earn a safe sum which will be awarded to you if you were to fail on this particular stage. You wull have access to 3 jokers which you can use at any time.

To win the game you have to reach the final stage and answer the last question correctly!
